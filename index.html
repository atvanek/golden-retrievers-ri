Who We Are

We are a family-run business that has an extensive history of hand-raising healthy, well-behaved, beautiful Golden-Retrievers. Based in Rhode Island, we pride ourselves in maintaing the highest quality of life for our 4 adult Golden Retrievers, whom we treat like our own children.

We raise our dogs with the highest quality food, medical care, and enrichment. Our goldens have amazing temperments for families or anyone who's looking for a loyal, life-long, loving companion.

Each of our puppies comes with

Health Guarantee

The health and pedigree of our puppies is assessed by a veterinarian and is guaranteed or your money back

9-way Vaccine

This vaccine includes

  • Parvovirus
  • Distemper
  • Adenovirus

Microchip

Each puppy comes with a microchip and microchip number that can be used by an veterinary clinic or hospital to find the owner

Deworm Treatment
